Working Together For Change


At WTFC, we believe that it is critical to think not only of ourselves but also of others. We are a student-led network creating a large student community to help homeless people throughout the United States. There is a lot of work being done by many organizationsâ€”but nothing truly led by students. Working Together for Change is a national student-led nonprofit dedicated to helping the homelessnes.


IMMERSION

The Immersion pillar works to provide our volunteers with a more realistic understanding of what it means to experience homelessness firsthand. Our programs bring together students from all backgrounds to help empathize with the homeless rather than just sympathize with them.

MEDICAL

Homeless men and women are more likely than the average person to get sick. That's why we place heavy emphasis on our medical initiative. Several times throughout the year, we set up health camps and/or hold fairs in various locations throughout Georgia.

ENTREPRENEURSHIP

Social entrepreneurship is the implementation of sustainable, community-based solutions aimed at ending homelessness one person at a time. We provide scholarships, job training, and opportunities to start and manage local businesses.
