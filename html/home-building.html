Building a Home

The Silicon Valley Chapter of WTFC went to Mexico to build a house for a family. Over the course of four days, we traveled down to Tijuana, built a home, and settled a family in it. This year, we join 10 families from Aug. 12thâ€“ 15th to make this house a reality for a family.

Our Plan

All lodging, food, and local transportation costs are shared among all the participants.

In 4 days, we complete building the entire house from cutting wood, building walls, painting, decorating, and furnishing!

The best part is the giant smile on the faces of the family and the children!
