Who We Are

At Working Together for Change, our end goal is to end homelessness, one person at a time. Initially, when our co-founders Nitish and Aditya Sood began their work against homelessness, they quickly noticed there was quite a lot of work being done by many fantastic organizations – but nothing really included the students. Working Together for Change is a student-led nonprofit that serves as a platform to leverage this key demographic – students – to help end homelessness.
